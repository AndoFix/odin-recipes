Haggis

Ingredients

Serves 8

  • 1 sheep stomach
  • ¼lb or 113g of suet
  • 2lb or 907g of sheep's liver, heart and lungs
  • ½ or 227g of lamb kidney
  • 3 peeled yellow onions
  • 1 teaspoon of dried corriander
  • ½ teaspoon of mace (or nutmeg)
  • 1lb or 450g of oatmeal
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Method

Step 1

Wash sheep's stomach and gently score the inside.

Step 2

Soak the stomach in saly water for 8 hours.

Step 3

Wash liver, heart, lungs, kidney and fat. Place offal in boiling brine. Cover and cook slowly for 2 hours.

Step 4

Drain the offal and stomach. Remove trachea and cartlage and chop finely.

Step 5

Place onions in a large pot and blanch them for 10 minutes.

Step 6

Drain the onions but keep the water.

Step 7

Roast the oatmeal in a skillet on low unitl crisp.

Step 8

Mix all the Ingredients together by adding the onion water until firm
knead for 5 minutes.

Step 9

Fill the stomach with the stuffing until ⅔ full.

Step 10

Remove the air from the stomach and tie both ends

Step 11

Puncture the stomach with a needle several times to avoid bursting.

Step 12

Place haggis in a large covered pot of boiling water for 3½ minutes.

Step 13

Cut the strings at both ends.

Step 14

Serve by opening the stomach and placing stuffing on a plate with whatever you like.
Alternatively you could just throw it in the bin and order out.
