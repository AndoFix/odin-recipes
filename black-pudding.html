Black Pudding

Ingredients

Makes roughly 3 lbs or 1.5 kg

  • 4 cups of fresh pig's blood
  • 2½ teaspoons of salt
  • 1½ cups of steel-cut oatmeal
  • 2 cups of finely diced pork fat finely chopped
  • l large yellow onion finely chopped
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1½ teaspoons of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of allspice

Method

Step 1

Preheat an oven to 160°C or 325°F. Either grease two glass bread pans or line metal pans with baking paper.
Note: If metal pans are not lined the metal will react with the blood and ruin the flavour.

Step 2

Boil 2½ cups of water and stir in oats. Simmer while stirring occasionally for 15 minutes.
Note: Remove from heat when oats are just tender.

Step 3

Stir 1 teaspoon of salt into the blood. Pour blood through a fine sieve to remove lumps. Add fat, onions, milk, pepper,
allspice and the remaining salt. Add the oatmeal and mix. Add mixture to bread pans and cover with foil and bake
for an hour until it's firm.
