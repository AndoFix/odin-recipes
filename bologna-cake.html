Bologna Cake

Ingredients

  • Bologna, about a pound or half a kilogram
  • Softened cream cheese
  • Onion powder

Method

Step 1

Mix the onion powder into the softened cream cheese.

Step 2

Dry bologna with a paper towel and place the first slice on a plate.

Step 3

Spread a teaspoon of softened cream cheese over the bologna slice and repeat these steps until the cake is the desired height.

Step 4

Spread the remaining cream cheese over the pile of bologna until it looks like a real cake. Whipped cream can be added to make it look more appealing.

Note: Bologna can be substituted for any deli meat as long as they are thick slices.
